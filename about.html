About Us

At Santa Cruz Bicycles, we are committed to building the most advanced, high-performance mountain bikes in the world. Founded in 1994 in Santa Cruz, California, our company was born from a desire to create bikes that could handle the toughest trails with precision, durability, and speed. Over the years, we have remained at the forefront of innovation, pioneering industry-leading designs like our Virtual Pivot Point (VPP) suspension system, which delivers exceptional traction, efficiency, and control on all terrain.

Every Santa Cruz bike is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using the highest-quality carbon fiber and aluminum to ensure strength without compromising weight. Our in-house carbon manufacturing process allows us to create frames that are not only incredibly durable but also tailored for elite-level performance. Whether it’s a world-championship-winning downhill race bike, a cutting-edge enduro machine, or a trail bike built for all-day adventures, we engineer every model to exceed expectations.

Beyond performance, we are dedicated to sustainability and longevity. Our Lifetime Warranty on frames and bearings reflects our commitment to quality, and our Santa Cruz PayDirt Initiative invests in trail-building projects worldwide to support the riding community. With a deep-rooted passion for mountain biking and an unwavering drive for excellence, Santa Cruz Bicycles continues to push the limits of what’s possible on two wheels. Ride Santa Cruz and experience the difference.
